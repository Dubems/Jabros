I. Introduction I am pleased to submit proposal for the redesigning of howtogrowfinance.com website. I welcome the anticipated opportunity to work with How to Grow Finance on this project. The emergence of the internet era enables the company to expand its reach to its target audience as well as serve its existing customers in a far more effective way. It is our understanding that How to Grow Finance seeks to update the design and structure of its website to improve the organization and presentation of the content provided so that visitors can quickly navigate to desired pages and to eliminate the current confusion. I believe am highly qualified to assist and guide the company through a comprehensive website overhaul. II. Current problems. After critical analysis of the company’s current website, below are my findings. 1. The website has no favicon: A favicon is a picture (preferably the company’s logo specially designed) located at the browser’s tab. This helps for easy navigation of visitors to the company’s website. The pictures below shows clearly what I mean. 2. The website is not Mobile responsive: It’s not news that the world is going mobile with the invention of smaller and smaller powerful devices, hence the need for the company’s website to be mobile responsive. This makes the customers who visit the site having to zoom in and out sliding left and right to view the website content. 3. The website content is not well structured: The website content is not well arranged, hence it doesn’t give a good user experience. For example, it may take a visitor to the website some time to locate the navigation bar (where the HOME, ABOUT US… is located). 4. The website is not dynamic: The website needs to be more dynamic, having siders, Transitions this makes the visitor excited and want to know more. III.
